Who I Am
Built to solve.
Wired to lead.

I am a Chemical Engineering graduate from KNUST who chose to apply engineering thinking to institutions — not just factories. Over the past five years, I have led operations, quality systems, and academic programmes across manufacturing, education, and the church sector in Ghana.

At OpenLabs Ghana, I serve as Head of Academics — designing workflows, managing facilitators, building KPI systems, and aligning programme delivery with strategic goals. Before that, I ran the full operations of Novelty Montessori School for nearly three years, from staffing and budgeting to vendor management and compliance.

Alongside my operational career, I build custom digital tools — management systems for schools and churches, data dashboards, and analytics deliverables — using Node.js, MongoDB, JavaScript, and Excel. I believe technology and operations are two sides of the same coin.

I am ISO-certified, Lean Six Sigma White Belt trained, and constantly expanding my capability in data analytics and project management. I bring both the technical rigour of an engineer and the warmth of someone who genuinely cares about the people he works with.
